Biography

Alizée Jacotey born in Ajaccio on 21 August 1984 is a French recording artist. Following her winning performance in the talent show "Graines de Star" in 1999, she collaborated with collaborating with Mylène Farmer and Laurent Boutonnat.

Alizée entered the music business in 2000. She was the highest selling female French singer in 2001 when she released "Gourmandises". "Mes Courants Électriques" was released in 2003. During the fall of 2003 Alizée performed in 43 concerts throughout France, Belgium and Switzerland.

In Las Vegas, Nevada, USA, Alizée married J. Chatelain on 6 November 2003, with whom she has a daughter, Annily, born to them 27 April 2005. After a time spent out of the media limelight, Alizée returned in December 2007 with a new album entitled "Psychédélices". Her fourth album titled "Une Enfant Du Siècle" was released on 29 March 2010.
